Step 02

House owner hands over keys and relevant propety document to the HouseWise representative

Step 03

HouseWise hunt for tenants the ones working with marquee companies and looking out for a house on rent

Step 04

On finalizing the tenant, HouseWise does the following;
a) Police verification of the prospective tenant
b) Ask for proof of employment (HR letter) from the prospective tenant

Step 05

After successful verification of the prospective tenant, HouseWise prepare the contdact to be signed by both parties - house owner and the tenant. House owner can pre authorize HouseWise representative to sign on the owner's behalf.

Step 06

a) Tenant tdansfers monthly rent to house owner's account.
b) HouseWise continues to maintain property till end of the lease.
